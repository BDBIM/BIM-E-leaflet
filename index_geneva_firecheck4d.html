ABOUT

What is FireCheck 4D?

The FireCheck 4D revolutionises the design and regulatory process by implementing Building Information Modelling (BIM) automated checking functions for compliance with fire safety requirements, enabling precise spatial analysis as well as material tracking for design flexibility and certainty.

It executes a standardised and automated regulatory process, transforming design and checking workflow from manual to automated, through creating machine-readable rules.

By bridging digital precision with physical assurance, we are building a world where every building stands resilient from concept to legacy.

Design & analysis

Simulate and analyse for statutory compliance

IFC Data Schema

Data schema and parameter setting make space, use, evacuation sequence, building elements and construction machine-readable. Our unique data structure successfully extracts essential data from BIM model for automated computation and analysis.

Evacuation Sequence Analysis

Evacuation sequence analysis is inherently complex, as building escape routes form a matrix of possible scenarios. By extracting the egress direction of each opening between consecutive spaces, an 'in-out' logic is established.

This logic links all spaces along the evacuation path within the BIM model, enabling automated determination of people flow sequences. Such analysis is critical for evaluating travel distances, cumulative population in each space, and the required width and number of escape routes or staircases.

Space to Space Approach

Hong Kong Fire Safety Code stipulates that the width of each exit route, as well as the combined width of all exit routes, must be determined based on occupancy capacity and the number of exits provided. The 'in-out' logic supports this process by computing the possible maximum population in each individual space, ensuring that exit widths are sufficient for safe evacuation.

Travel Distance Assessment

Unlike paper submission which relies on manual judgment and measurement of travel distances, we have adopted various geometrical principles and developed our own pathfinding algorithms to automate the travel distance assessment.

Fire Resisting Construction

In Hong Kong, every building should be divided into fire compartments by fire barriers to inhibit the spread of fire. By extracting compartment information and building element data, the integrity and continuity of these fire separations will be automatically assessed.

Firefighting and Rescue

By delineating emergency vehicular access in the BIM model, spatial analysis will be conducted to ensure the envelope of the building is adequately served by fire appliance.

Certitude de construction

Fire Rated Material Traceability

By providing a digital linkage between design and on-site construction, a reliable system is established which tracks critical material attributes including specifications, suppliers, sampling and test results.

We link BIM models with RFID technology to verify every critical fire safety element is "Built-to-Design". This guarantees flawless execution on-site, eliminating common construction pitfalls that compromise fire safety.

Certitude du cycle de vie

Alterations and Additions

Before implementation of alterations and additions works, the impact of the proposed changes can be simulated for assessment, such as extensions increasing compartment size, use changes raising population loads, or repartitioning affecting travel distances.

This predictive approach spots out possible contraventions and ensures the proposed changes are compatible to fire safety design of the building.

SMART FEATURES

The FireCheck 4D is equipped with a series of smart functions to facilitate data entry and plan preparation, including automatic filling of essential parameters based on room use, identification of missing parameters and data irregularities and systematic import of the checking result schedules back to the BIM file for preparation and processing of plan submissions.
