Practitioners' Pain points

Seeking a quick and practical tool that allows design flexibility

  • Current practice heavily relies on 2D projection, which may overestimate blockage between buildings and lacks 3D detail.
  • Although 3D Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) offers an alternative, its simulation and analysis are time-consuming, leading to missing the golden period for testing design options.
